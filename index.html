Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2024

At 3addah, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you send us a message on WhatsApp or request on-demand services. This information may include: name, phone number, and delivery address.

2. Location Information

When you use 3addah for delivery, we collect precise location data about the delivery. If you share your location via WhatsApp, we use that data to facilitate the pickup and drop-off.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Facilitate payments and send receipts.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share the information we collect about you with delivery partners to enable them to provide the services you request. For example, we share your name and delivery address with the driver who picks up your order.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
